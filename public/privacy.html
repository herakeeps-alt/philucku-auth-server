1. Introduction

Welcome to Philucky ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Service").

Important Notice: This app is designed for educational purposes to help users learn about casino games. No real money transactions occur within this app. For actual gaming with real money, please visit our main website.

By using the Philucky app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect the following information when you register for an account:

  • Phone Number: Required for account creation and login
  • Email Address: Optional, for account recovery and communications
  • Username: Your chosen display name
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Which guides you view, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed
  • App Performance Data: Crash reports, diagnostic information

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
  • Financial information (no payments in this app)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contact lists or photos
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate your identity when you log in
  • To provide access to educational gaming guides
  • To personalize your learning experience

3.2 Communication

  • To send account-related notifications
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To send updates about new guides or features (with your consent)

3.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • To analyze app usage and improve our Service
  • To develop new features and content
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • To ensure platform security

3.4 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To protect against fraud and abuse

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard security measures:

4.1 Technical Measures

  • Encryption: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
  • Secure Transmission: Data transmitted over HTTPS/SSL protocols
  • Authentication: JWT-based secure authentication system
  • Database Security: MongoDB with access controls and encryption at rest

4.2 Administrative Measures

  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Account approval process to prevent unauthorized access
Please Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account at any time.
